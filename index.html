MIG Over Time

Founded in 1981 by Mr. Wagdy Karrar, the MIG family of companies is one of the industry’s leading real estate investment and development firms, with experience in owning, developing, and operating assets across multiple industry segments.

​MIG originally worked in construction, and over the years the company have successfully built, developed, and executed a number of strategies, which has resulted in the successful execution of several portfolios and operating platform transactions that have helped build MIG’s reputation and track record which laid the foundation for the company’s future growth and expansion.
